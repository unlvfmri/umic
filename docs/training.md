!!! warning "Required Training"

    All personnel must complete safety training via CHIMERA before entering UMIC.
	
!!! note "Supervision Requirement"

    To ensure the highest standards of participant safety, independent scanning is not permitted. All sessions must be conducted under the direct, on-site supervision of a staff MRI Technologist.

---

### Step 1: Access CHIMERA

- UNLV Students/Staff: Log in with your ACE credentials. Please follow the instructions in the 
<a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/1emWP_YpSqA9TVJtlGzgKU4Moq2o4vTWT/view" target="_blank">
Accessing CHIMERA Guide
</a>.

- External Users: Register for a <a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/1RHfvcfUpATixuMJa7EtzEDgO5AX6AOQk/view" target="_blank">CHIMERA account</a> and complete the MRI Safety Online Training Module.

---

### Step 2: Access Levels

Access is granted in three tiers based on your training and experience:

#### Non-MR Personnel
- Complete online module  
- Pass quiz (≥ 90% or 9/10)

#### Level 1
- Complete online module  
- Pass quiz (≥ 90% or 27/30)

#### Level 2
- Successfully pass the Level 1 exam  
- Complete **10+ hours** of supervised training, hands-on clinical training.  
- Receive final safety sign-off from a **Level 3 MRI Technologist**

---

### Annual Recertification

All MR safety certifications for non-MR personnel, Level 1 and Level 2 personnel are valid for a period of one (1) calendar year from the date of the most recent assessment and must be renewed annually.
