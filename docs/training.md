## Safety Training

!!! warning "Required Training"

    All personnel must complete safety training via CHIMERA before entering UMIC.

### Step 1: Access CHIMERA

- UNLV Students/Staff: Log in with your ACE credentials

- External Users: Register for a CHIMERA account and complete MRI Safety Online Training

### Step 2: Access Levels

Access is granted in three tiers:

#### Non-MR Personnel
- Complete online module  
- Pass quiz (≥ 90%)

#### Level 1
- Complete online module  
- Pass quiz (≥ 90%)

#### Level 2
- Pass Level 1 exam  
- Complete 10+ hours supervised training  
- Final approval from Level 3 MRI Technologist

### Annual Recertification

All certifications are valid for 1 year and must be renewed annually.
