### Step 1: Access CHIMERA

- UNLV Students/Staff: Log in with your ACE credentials. Please follow the instructions in the <a href="https://docs.google.com/document/d/1mG5HNleDykL2ZUtjCZe5qIR4otLK0Rt95eiplc0mFJY/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.g6hfeotnq34a" target="_blank">Acessing CHIMERA Guide</a>.

- External Users: Register for a <a href="https://docs.google.com/document/d/1iLnn5CHXHHOLwAda5DKGpQJKZulKUyemUeZ-5QUXI9A/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.f7i6mqnw7vmq" target="_blank">CHIMERA account</a> and complete the MRI Safety Online Training Module.

---

### Step 2: Access Levels

Access is granted in three tiers based on your training and experience:

#### Non-MR Personnel
- Complete online module  
- Pass quiz (≥ 90% or 9/10)

#### Level 1
- Complete online module  
- Pass quiz (≥ 90% or 27/30)

#### Level 2
- Successfully pass the Level 1 exam  
- Complete **10+ hours** of supervised training, hands-on clinical training.  
- Receive final safety sign-off from a **Level 3 MRI Technologist**

---

### Annual Recertification

All MR safety certifications for non-MR personnel, Level 1 and Level 2 personnel are valid for a period of one (1) calendar year from the date of the most recent accessment and must be renewed annually.
