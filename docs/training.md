\# Training



\## Safety Training



All personnel must complete safety training via CHIMERA before entering UMIC.



\### Step 1: Access CHIMERA



\- UNLV Students/Staff: Log in with your credentials  

\- External Users: Register for a CHIMERA account and complete MRI Safety Online Training



\### Step 2: Access Levels



Access is granted in 3 tiers:



\- \*\*Non-MR Personnel\*\*

&#x20; - Complete online module

&#x20; - Pass quiz (≥90%)



\- \*\*Level 1\*\*

&#x20; - Complete module

&#x20; - Pass quiz (≥90%)



\- \*\*Level 2\*\*

&#x20; - Pass Level 1 exam  

&#x20; - Complete 10+ hours supervised training  

&#x20; - Final approval from Level 3 MRI Technologist  



\### Annual Recertification



All certifications are valid for 1 year and must be renewed annually.
